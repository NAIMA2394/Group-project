about us

Welcome to Sagalee Joint! We are passionate
about exporing and sharing the vibrant street
food culture in Kenya. From bustline city
markets to local street coners, Kenyan street
food offers an incredible variety of falvours,
aromas, and stories that showcase the heart of
our culinary heritage.Our mission is to bring
this experiences to life and provide a platform
where food enthusiast can learn, dicover, and
celebrate Kenya's Unique street food services

What we Do

We dive deep into the world of Kenyan street
food to bring you authentic stories, recipes,
and guieds. Whether its a guide to the best
street food spots, profiles on local venders, or a
closer llok at the ingredients that makes these
dishes unique, we aim to provide a rich
immersive experiences. through our platforms,
you will not only find delicious street food
recommendations but also the stories behind the
food and the people who bring it to life.
